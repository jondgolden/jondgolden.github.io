about me

I’m a UX designer with over 10 years of experience creating intuitive, user-centered experiences across digital and physical platforms. I focus on translating research, business requirements, and complex systems into clear user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity designs that prioritize usability and clarity.
With a background that spans UX, visual design, and print, I bring a holistic perspective to problem-solving and collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to deliver thoughtful, scalable solutions that align user needs with business goals. When I’m not working, you’ll find me outdoors around Chattanooga—on my mountain bike, hiking with my wife, or checking out a new local restaurant.
