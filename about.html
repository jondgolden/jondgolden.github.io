about me

I am a creative professional with more than 10 years of experience. As a native from the Chattanooga area, I pull inspiration from the endless beauty this area offers. I am well-versed in both print and digital media, but I specialize in print design. When I'm not working or reading about the latest design trends, you can find me in nature with my wife sailing through the woods on my mountain bike, hiking with my pup, or checking out a local brewery.
